## 🎮 JAR IN MINECRAFT NUTZEN

1. **Kopiere die JAR:**
   ```
   build/libs/cyberware-1.0.0.jar
   ```

2. **Füge zu Minecraft Mods hinzu:**
   - Windows: `%appdata%\.minecraft\mods\`
   - Linux: `~/.minecraft/mods/`
   - Mac: `~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/mods/`

3. **Minecraft starten:**
   - Forge Profile wählen
   - Play!
